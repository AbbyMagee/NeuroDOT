Readme: Age Specific Head Atlases

Four age-specific head atlases are included in this folder: NeuroDOT/Support_Files/Atlases:

1. 6-month Atlas
	-6mo_4layer_Mask.mat
	-6moAtlas_3mm.mat
2. 24-month Atlas
	-24mo_4layer_Mask.mat
	-24moAtlas_3mm.mat
3. 36-month Atlas
	-36mo_4layer_Mask.mat
	-36moAtlas_3mm.mat
4. 60-month Atlas
	-60mo_4layer_Mask.mat
	-60moAtlas_3mm.mat

These flies are packaged in .mat format, though they contain the information from the original NIfTI files.
Each of these files contains a "mask" and an "infoT1" variable. These variables are the volume and header associated with the atlas. 

Along with these atlases, the EEG 10-20 and 10-5 coordinates and high-density head meshes are provided in the following files:
1. 6-month Atlas
	-ten5_6mo.mat
	-ten20_6mo.mat
	-meshHD_6mo.mat - available from NITRC
2. 24-month Atlas
	-ten5_24mo.mat
	-ten20_24mo.mat
	-meshHD_24mo.mat - available from NITRC
3. 36-month Atlas
	-ten5_36mo.mat
	-ten20_36mo.mat
	-meshHD_36mo.mat - available from NITRC
4. 60-month Atlas
	-ten5_60mo.mat
	-ten20_60mo.mat
	-meshHD_60mo.mat - available from NITRC
